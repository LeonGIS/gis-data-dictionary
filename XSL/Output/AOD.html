Arts Overlay District

Download Layer

  • Open Data

Summary

To provide a boundary of the Arts Overlay District (AOD), as depicted on the Zoning Map on file with the Town Clerk.

Description

The Arts Overlay District (AOD) is established to encourage the development, preservation and enhancement of Arts-Related Uses, particularly within East Dedham. The district shall preserve and enhance the area as a center for a variety of retail, business services, housing, and office uses and promote a strong pedestrian character and scale throughout the district.Development in the AOD shall be governed by the Dedham Zoning Bylaw underlying zoning district except when developing arts-related uses.
